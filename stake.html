Delegating to a staking pool is a simple way for CCD holder to earn rewards without having to run a node or become a validator. When you delegate some CCD to AURO.node, it increases our chance being selected to produce a block and getting rewards, which are then shared with you.

You can delegate CCD in your Concordium Wallet for Web. If you don't have one, look at the instruction.

When you delegates an amount of CCD to Auro.Node, the CCD are not transferred to the validator and remain under your control. Staked CCD, both for delegators and validators, cannot be spent while staked.

Please take a look at our step by step guide on How to delegate CCD in the Concordium Wallet for Web. On the "Register delegation" screen enter the Baker ID 89706.
